KEY FEATURES
================================================================================

1. TICKETING SYSTEM
   - Create and assign tasks
   - Manage queries
   - Track status (open, in progress, resolved, closed)
   - Priority levels (low, medium, high, urgent)

2. PATIENT ASSIGNMENT
   - Assign patients to doctors
   - Bulk assignment
   - Track doctor workload
   - View unassigned patients

3. DEPARTMENT & TEAM MANAGEMENT
   - Create departments
   - Assign department heads
   - Create specialized teams (Clinical, Emergency, Surgical, Pediatric, ICU, etc.)
   - Assign team leads and members

4. AI CLINICAL ASSISTANT (Doctors only)
   - Patient history summary
   - Lab results analysis
   - Treatment recommendations
   - Risk factor assessment
   - Clinical question answering

5. DOCTOR NOTES
   - Clinical notes on patients
   - Note types: consultation, diagnosis, treatment, follow-up, observation
   - Private notes option

6. HEALTH CHATBOT (Dr. Tega)
   - Available to ALL users
   - General health questions
   - Health tips (nutrition, exercise, mental health, sleep, preventive care)
   - Medical condition explanations
   - 24/7 availability

7. PERSONAL DIARY
   - Available to ALL users
   - Text entries
   - Audio recordings
   - Video recordings
   - Mood tracking
   - Tags and organization
   - Completely private

8. PATIENT MEDICAL HISTORY
   - Upload medical files (lab results, scans, reports, prescriptions)
   - Categorize files (blood test, X-ray, MRI, CT scan, etc.)
   - Add descriptions and notes
   - Search and filter files
   - Track who uploaded what and when
   - Download files
   - Available to: Doctors, Nurses, Lab Techs, Pharmacists, Admins

9. PRESCRIPTION MANAGEMENT
   - Doctors create prescriptions
   - Pharmacists dispense medications
   - Track prescription status (pending/dispensed)
   - Add dispensing notes
   - Search by patient or medication
   - Complete audit trail
   - Available to: Pharmacists, Doctors, Admins

10. SUBSCRIPTION & BILLING SYSTEM
   - 60-day automatic trial period for all hospitals
   - Monthly plan: ₦150,000/month
   - Yearly plan: ₦1,400,000/year (save ₦400,000)
   - Hospital admins manage subscriptions and payments
   - System admins monitor all subscriptions and adjust pricing
   - Automatic expiration alerts (7 days before)
   - Payment history tracking
   - Revenue statistics for system admin
   - Only hospital admins see billing (other staff don't)

11. PHARMACY INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
   - Complete medication stock tracking
   - Categories: antibiotics, painkillers, antivirals, vitamins, etc.
   - Dosage forms: tablets, capsules, syrups, injections, creams
   - Stock levels with reorder alerts
   - Expiry date tracking with alerts
   - Batch number and supplier tracking
   - Stock adjustments (restock, dispense, expired, adjustment)
   - Transaction history for audit trail
   - Inventory value calculations
   - Low stock alerts
   - Location tracking (shelf/cabinet)
   - Available to: Pharmacists, Hospital Admins

12. BILLING & PAYMENTS SYSTEM (Phase 1)
   - Service pricing management (61 pre-configured services)
   - Auto-charge system (charges added automatically from all departments)
   - Real-time patient billing
   - Itemized invoices with detailed breakdown
   - Multiple payment methods (cash, card, transfer, mobile money)
   - Receipt generation and printing
   - Discount management with approval workflow:
     * <10%: Accountant
     * 10-30%: Accounts Manager
     * >30%: Hospital Admin
   - Payment processing and tracking
   - Financial reports (daily, monthly, annual)
   - Revenue analytics by department/service
   - Outstanding balance tracking
   - Complete audit trail
   - Available to: Accountants, Accounts Managers, Hospital Admins
   
   Service Categories:
